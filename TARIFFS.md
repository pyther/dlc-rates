# Duquesne Light Company (DLC) Historic Tariff Archive

This repository maintains an archive of historical tariff filings and supplements for Duquesne Light Company. These documents are provided for personal reference and historical tracking of electric rate changes in the region.

**Notice:** These are copies of public filings made with the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission (PUC). For official, authoritative, and up-to-date rate information, please visit the [Duquesne Light Company Official Rates Page](https://duquesnelight.com/rates).
